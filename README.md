# 🥊 bonboxing

The whole town is after you — shops, factories, die Post and the Steueramt, all firing receipts at your face. Scan them the hard way.

**Play it: https://yannickpulver.github.io/bonboxing/**

![bonboxing gameplay](screenshot.png)

## How to play

- **Left click** — left jab
- **Right click** — right cross
- **Mouse** — aim
- **A/D** or **←/→** — sidestep
- **M** — mute

Punch the floating cards for power-ups. Beware the Steuerrechnung.

## Tech

Single `index.html` — Three.js scene, Web Audio soundtrack, no build step.
